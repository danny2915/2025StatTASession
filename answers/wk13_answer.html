Shangri-La Frontier is a cutting-edge VRMMO game. After a major update, Sunraku is testing a new build and wants to know whether it actually increases his average damage per combo compared to his old build, which had a known average of 1200 damage per combo in similar areas.

Sunraku goes to a stable grinding spot and records the damage per combo for 15 battles using the new build:

\[ 1310, 1195, 1240, 1285, 1220, 1350, 1290, 1210, 1265, 1305, 1330, 1275, 1255, 1190, 1320 \]

  1. Run an appropriate one-sample t-test using t.test(), and report a 95% confidence interval for the mean damage with the form: 95 percent confidence interval: (a, b).
damage_sunraku <- c(
  1310, 1195, 1240, 1285, 1220,
  1350, 1290, 1210, 1265, 1305,
  1330, 1275, 1255, 1190, 1320
)

# Test if mean > 1200
t.test(
  damage_sunraku,
  mu = 1200,
  alternative = "greater",
  conf.level = 0.95
)
